About Us

Our Council helps students get better at programming through organizing coding contests and hackathons. It is majorly aimed at promoting the interdisciplinary nature of coding amongst students, irrespective of their field. We organize various events for the same on intra and inter college level as well as national level

Our Events

We provide assistance to coding enthusiasts by conducting innovative and fun challenges, hackathons & interactive and informative webinars throughout the year
